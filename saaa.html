SAN ANDREAS AVIATION ADMINISTRATION
The San Andreas Aviation Administration (SAAA) serves as the central authority governing all aviation activities across the state.

As the regulatory body, SAAA establishes aviation policies, ensures compliance with safety standards, and oversees the operations of agencies like Los Santos Aviation (LSA).

SAAA is committed to maintaining secure, efficient, and well-regulated airspace. From certifying aviation institutions to supervising aircraft operations and enforcing state-wide aviation laws, the administration works to ensure that every flight meets the highest standards of safety and professionalism.
