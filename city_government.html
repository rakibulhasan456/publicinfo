CITY OF LOS SANTOS
The City Government represents the elected leadership of the government. It is chosen directly by the people and is responsible for managing public administration, economic development, business regulation, and citywide operations.

The City Government ensures that all companies and organizations operate within laws and maintain fairness for all citizens.

The Mayor's Office works as the executive body of government, carrying out policies, enforcing regulations, and maintaining transparency and accountability. It acts as the bridge between the public and the government’s administrative departments.

LEGISLATION
The City Government is responsible for creating and enforcing legislation that ensures fair business practices, protects citizens, and supports economic growth.

These laws define how businesses operate, how companies are formed, and how public services are regulated. This legislation provides a structured legal framework that maintains order, promotes transparency, and ensures all organizations follow approved standards and policies.

▸ Los Santos Real Estate and Renter Protection Act
CITY GOVERNMENT RESPONSIBILITY
The City Government is responsible for overseeing all business and company operations within the city. It manages registrations, approvals, regulations, and ensures that every organization follows state laws and operates fairly.
