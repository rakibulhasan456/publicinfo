GOVERNMENT
The Government oversees the administration, development, and regulation of the entire country. It is led by the President, who serves as the highest executive authority and ensures smooth coordination between all branches and departments.

The Government is divided into three main wings: Mayor's Office (Elected Branch), Governor's Office (Appointed Branch), and the President's Cabinet (Appointed Branch), each handling different responsibilities while working together to maintain stability, public service, and economic growth.
MAYOR'S OFFICE
The Mayor's Office is the elected wing of the government. Its leadership is chosen directly by the people and is responsible for managing all business, company operations, and state-wide administrative matters.

It is led by the Mayor, supported by the Deputy Mayor, the City Manager, and City Advisor.

Together, the elected leadership ensures transparent governance, fair regulation of businesses, and effective public service across the entire state of San Andreas.
Mayor's Office Administration
GOVERNOR'S OFFICE
The Governor's Office is the appointed wing of the government. Its leadership is chosen by the president and is responsible for managing all state-wide license and regulations.

It is led by the Governor, supported by the Vice Governor, the State Manager, and State Advisor.

Together, the State Government ensures consistent availability of licenses and regulatory approvals to all citizens across the entire state of San Andreas.

THE PRESIDENT'S CABINET
The Cabinet is composed of the Department Heads from major government sectors. Unlike the elected Executive Office, Cabinet members are selected by the President, based on merit, experience, and leadership ability, and serve at the President's discretion.

The Cabinet consists of the heads of:

● Department of Justice (DOJ)
● San Andreas Aviation Administration (SAAA)

These appointed leaders manage their respective Federal departments, enforce regulations, and ensure the delivery of cohesive public service. Their selection by the President guarantees professional oversight and specialized expertise in each key sector, maintaining high standards across the administration.
